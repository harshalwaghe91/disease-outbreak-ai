## AI Features

### AI-Based Disease Outbreak Prediction

The core of OutbreakAI is a Machine Learning prediction engine built using the Random Forest algorithm. The model analyzes patient demographics, symptoms, vital signs, and laboratory test results to estimate disease risk and identify possible disease categories.

### Intelligent Risk Assessment

Instead of providing only a prediction, the system evaluates the severity level and classifies patients into:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

This helps users understand the urgency of medical attention required.

### Automated Pattern Recognition

The AI model identifies hidden patterns in healthcare data such as:

* Elevated White Blood Cell (WBC) count
* Increased CRP levels
* Reduced Oxygen Saturation
* Low Platelet Count
* Fever-related abnormalities

These patterns are used to generate outbreak risk predictions.

### Predictive Healthcare Analytics

The analytics dashboard transforms prediction data into visual insights, helping users identify:

* Disease distribution trends
* Risk-level statistics
* Prediction frequency
* Population-level outbreak patterns

### AI-Powered Recommendation Engine

Based on prediction outcomes, the system automatically provides preventive recommendations and health awareness guidance.

### Smart Consultation Chatbot

The integrated chatbot offers general health consultation, symptom guidance, and emergency escalation support. It can recognize critical keywords such as:

* Chest Pain
* Breathing Difficulty
* Unconsciousness
* Severe Bleeding

and immediately advise urgent medical assistance.

### Explainable AI Approach

Unlike black-box systems, OutbreakAI uses interpretable machine learning techniques that allow users to understand how health indicators influence risk predictions.
